Wild Mushroom Gnocchi

Description

Indulge in a culinary symphony of earthy flavors with a Wild Mushroom Gnocchi dish. This dish begins with tender potato gnocchi, cooked to perfection and then bathed in a luxurious creamy sauce. The star of the show is a medley of wild mushrooms—porcini, chanterelle, and shiitake—sauteed with aromatic shallots and garlic until golden brown and irresistibly fragrant. As the mushrooms release their rich, savory essence, they meld harmoniously with the velvety cream sauce, creating a decadent coating that clings lovingly to each pillowy gnocchi. Finished with a generous sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and a touch of fresh parsley for brightness, this dish promises to enchant the palate with every luxurious bite, making it a true celebration of nature's bounty.

Ingredients:

  • 500g gnocchi
  • 300g mixed wild mushrooms(such as porchini, chanterelle, shiitake)
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 shallot, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup heavy creaam
  • 1/4 cup grated pamesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh Parsley

Steps:

  1. Cook the gnocchi according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the shallot and garlic, and sauté until fragrant, about 2 minutes.
  3. Add the wild mushrooms to the skillet and cook until they release their moisture and become tender, about 5-7 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and stir to combine. Let simmer for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add the cooked gnocchi to the skillet and toss to coat in the creamy mushroom sauce.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
