Kingklip with pineapple salsa and lemon butter sauce

Description:

Experience a burst of tropical flavors with our Kingklip accompanied by Pineapple Salsa and Lemon Butter Sauce. Succulent kingklip fillets, delicately seasoned and seared to golden perfection, offer a tender canvas for this vibrant culinary masterpiece. A zesty pineapple salsa, crafted from juicy diced pineapple, crisp red bell peppers, and tangy red onions, dances atop each fillet, infusing every bite with a symphony of sweet and savory notes. Complemented by a velvety lemon butter sauce, enriched with fragrant garlic and the bright essence of freshly squeezed lemon juice, this dish achieves a perfect harmony of flavors. With each forkful, the delicate sweetness of the pineapple salsa harmonizes elegantly with the rich, buttery sauce, creating a culinary experience that transports the senses to sun-kissed shores and tropical bliss. Prepare to delight in a symphony of taste sensations that will leave you longing for more.

Ingredients:

  • 4 kingklip fillets
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup diced pineapple
  • 1/4 cup diced red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • 4 kingklip fillets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

Steps:

  1. Season the kingklip fillets with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add the kingklip fillets and cook until golden brown and cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side.
  3. In a bowl, combine the diced pineapple, red bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, and lime juice to make the salsa. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. In a separate skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
  5. Stir in the lemon juice and continue to cook for another minute.
  6. To serve, place the cooked kingklip fillets on plates, top with pineapple salsa, and drizzle with lemon butter sauce.
